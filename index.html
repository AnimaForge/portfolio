AnimaReach – AI-Powered Outreach System

AnimaReach is a fully automated outreach solution designed for solopreneurs and micro-agencies. Built with n8n, OpenAI, and data enrichment APIs, it handles the entire pipeline: lead sourcing, message generation, and delivery — while preserving tone and trust. This project explores ethical automation, scalable relationship-building, and low-friction client acquisition.

IconCuts – Mobile Hair Service for Elderly Clients

A service platform and website offering dignified, in-home haircuts for senior citizens who may find it difficult to travel. The site is designed to build trust, highlight care, and simplify booking for families and facilities. This project combines social design, UX for vulnerable populations, and ethical service automation.

Verida – A New Model for Digital Mental Health

Verida is a concept project focused on reimagining digital mental health in Sweden by placing privacy, identity ownership, and empathy at the core. Inspired by the urgent need for safer, stigma-free access to therapy for young adults, the project explores how decentralized identity tools (DID, Verifiable Credentials ) could empower patients to control their therapeutic data while improving trust and access.

About Me

I'm a creative technologist with a deep interest in automation, symbolic systems, and the intersection between technology and personal transformation. My focus lies in building meaningful tools — not just functional ones — that enhance memory, identity, and decision-making.

I enjoy working with workflows (n8n, Notion, APIs), experimenting with AI systems, and designing interfaces that are both human-friendly and strategically optimized. I approach problems with a systems mindset and love exploring how seemingly disconnected parts can form elegant, purposeful wholes.

I’m currently studying Blockchain Development, while also building out a long-term personal project under the umbrella Anima Lux — a hybrid framework exploring the boundaries of automation, autonomy, and creative living. At the heart of my work is a simple idea: our systems should serve the soul, not the other way around.
